Daily Evening Prayer by the Satluj River ( Satluj Aarti)

The Satluj River, one of the longest rivers in the Indian subcontinent, holds immense historical and cultural significance, particularly in Hinduism. It is believed to be one of the five rivers of the Punjab region mentioned in the ancient texts of the Vedas. The river is often associated with the Saraswati, one of the most revered rivers in Hindu mythology. Historically, the Satluj River has been a lifeline for civilizations and has witnessed numerous significant events and settlements along its banks.
